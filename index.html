Il Paradiso Perduto

Italian Restaurant

Il Paradiso Perduto is located in the heart of Venice in the historic Cannaregio neighbourhood, the restaurant serves only the best and freshest seasonal produce, Italian cheeses, meat and seafood that reflect pure Italian flavours all prepared in an open kitchen in full view of diners. During the summer months the outdoor terrace provides the perfect view to Venice cannals.
